ABOUT CELL

Government Polytechnic College, Kaduthuruthy always had stood in the forefront in serving the societ as a technical institution providing quality skill education for the last 4 decades. The institution had contributed very much to the development of the locality as most of the alumni are employed with key role in technical departments of central, state and public sector establishments. The institute had contributed very high to the society. Established in the year 1999 . Polytechnic College under Department of Technical Education, Government of Kerala and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. With a built up area of over two hundred thousand square feet is situated in a sprawling campus of 3 acres, nestled among lush greenery, of kaduthuruthy Panchayath, in middle of kottayam District. Situated at the middle a peace and quiet area, the college provides a pleasant atmosphere for the students and is well connected. The infrastructure facilities are consistently being upgraded.The institution has an intake of 438 students annually in the Regular Diploma Programme in three domains computer Engineering Electronics and Communication Engineering Computer hardware Engineering
